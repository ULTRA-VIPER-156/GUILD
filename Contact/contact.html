FAQs

Have Questions?

We’re committed to making your experience as smooth and straightforward as possible.

What areas do you cover?

Our marketplace is currently available to students from [Specify Universities or Regions]. We are continuously expanding, so stay tuned for updates on new locations!

Can anyone sell on the platform?

No, only verified students can sell on the platform. During registration, sellers will be required to provide proof of student status (e.g., student ID or university email). This ensures a secure and student-friendly marketplace.

Can I return products??

Returns depend on the seller’s policy. Some sellers may allow returns, while others may not. If you choose the Buyer Protection payment option, you can request a refund if the item is not as described. Always check the product listing for the seller's return policy before making a purchase.

Are your products safe?

We strive to maintain a secure marketplace by verifying student sellers and encouraging transparency. While we do not physically inspect items, we provide a Buyer Protection option to ensure you receive what you paid for. We also encourage sellers to upload clear images and detailed descriptions of their products.

How do i make payment?

You can choose between two payment options: Buyer Protection or Direct Transfer.Buyer Protection: Your payment is held securely until you confirm receipt of the product in good condition. This ensures a safe transaction for both buyers and sellers.Direct Transfer: You can send the payment directly to the seller. However, this method does not offer the same level of security as Buyer Protection.
